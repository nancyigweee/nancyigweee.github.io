Target audience

My targeted audience for this website are residents who want to check for weather to plan out their daily activities.

Personas

Persona 1:

Name: James Brown

A clothing model.
Interested in fashion, and loves taking pictures.
Usually looksat the weather condition before getting ready for work since he'll be taking pictures outside.

Persona 2:

Name: Mercy Paul

A wedding planner.
Organizes and plans weddings for people looking to get married.
Checks the weather ahead of time to know the best location for weddings.

Scenarios

  1. The model trying to figure out the best time to take pictures on a specific day due to times for sunrise and sunset.
  2. The wedding planner deciding on whether the wedding should be planned for outside or inside.
  3. The model would like to know how warm or cold it will be to plan the outfits for a shoot.
  4. The wedding planner needs to check the weather to know the best time to plan the wedding in case it's too hot earlier or later in the day.
  5. Some other residents need to check the weather to decide what clothes to wear.
